FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I get lifetime access after purchasing a course?

Yes! Once you purchase a course, you have lifetime access including all future updates. Learn at your own pace without any time pressure.

Are the certificates recognized by employers?

Our certificates are recognized by hundreds of top companies worldwide. You can share them directly on LinkedIn or include them in your resume.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses. If you're not happy for any reason, just reach out and we'll process your refund within 2-3 business days.

Do I need any prior experience to start?

Most of our courses are beginner-friendly and start from the very basics. Each course page clearly states the prerequisites so you always know what to expect before enrolling.

How do I access the courses after purchasing?

After purchasing, courses are instantly available in your student dashboard. You can access them on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile — at any time.
