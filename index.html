About Indus Motor Company

Our Story

Driving Pakistan Forward

Indus Motor Company Ltd. (IMC) is a joint venture between the House of Habib, Toyota Motor Corporation Japan, and Toyota Tsusho Corporation. Incorporated in 1989, IMC manufactures and markets Toyota brand vehicles across Pakistan with a commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

1989

Company Founded

Indus Motor Company established as a joint venture in Karachi, Pakistan.

1993

First Vehicle Off the Line

Production began at Port Qasim plant — Corolla's iconic journey in Pakistan starts.

2005

Listed on Pakistan Stock Exchange

IMC becomes a publicly listed company, demonstrating strong financial growth.

2023

500,000 Vehicles Milestone

Celebrated over half a million vehicles delivered to Pakistani families.
