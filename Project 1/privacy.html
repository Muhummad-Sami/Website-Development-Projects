Privacy Policy

How Indus Motor Company collects, uses, and protects your information

Privacy Policy – Indus Motor Company Ltd.

Last updated: January 1, 2024  ·  Effective for all users of Toyota Pakistan's digital platforms and services.

1

Information We Collect: We collect personal information you provide when contacting us, registering for services, or submitting inquiries — including your name, CNIC, phone number, email address, and vehicle preferences.

2

How We Use Your Information: Your information is used to process inquiries, schedule test drives, provide after-sales support, send service reminders, and improve our products and services.

3

Data Sharing: We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Information may be shared with authorized Toyota dealerships, service centers, and Toyota Motor Corporation solely to fulfill your requests.

4

Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission.

5

Cookies and Tracking: Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain site features.

6

Marketing Communications: With your consent, we may send promotional messages about new vehicle launches, service offers, and events. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or clicking unsubscribe.

7

Retention of Data: We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by applicable Pakistani law, including the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA).

8

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, contact our Customer Relations team at customer.relations@toyota-indus.com.

9

Third-Party Links: Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

10

Children's Privacy: Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, please contact us immediately.

11

Policy Updates: Indus Motor Company reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12

Contact & Grievances: For any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: customer.relations@toyota-indus.com or call UAN +92-111-86-96-82 during business hours.

Policy accepted. Thank you!
