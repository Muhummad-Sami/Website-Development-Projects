About Indus Motor Company

Bringing Toyota's world-class quality to Pakistan since 1989

Our Story

Driving Pakistan Forward for Over 30 Years

Indus Motor Company Ltd. (IMC) is a joint venture between the House of Habib, Toyota Motor Corporation Japan, and Toyota Tsusho Corporation. Since our founding in 1989, we have been at the forefront of Pakistan's automotive industry — manufacturing and marketing world-class vehicles that Pakistani families trust every day.

Our plant in Port Qasim, Karachi, is equipped with the latest Japanese manufacturing technology and operates to Toyota's globally recognized quality standards. Every vehicle that rolls off our production line carries the promise of reliability, safety, and innovation.

With over 500,000 vehicles delivered and a network spanning more than 200 dealerships nationwide, we are proud to be Pakistan's most trusted automotive partner.

Our Mission

To provide customers with high-quality, reliable, and innovative vehicles while contributing to Pakistan's economic development and fostering sustainable mobility for future generations.

Our Vision

To be the leading automotive company in Pakistan, recognized for excellence in quality, customer satisfaction, and corporate responsibility.

Our Promise

Every Toyota we deliver is backed by our commitment to after-sales support, genuine parts, and a nationwide service network you can depend on.

Our Journey Through the Years
1989

Company Founded

Indus Motor Company Ltd. incorporated as a joint venture between House of Habib, Toyota Motor Corporation, and Toyota Tsusho Corporation.

1993

Production Begins

First vehicle rolls off the assembly line at our Port Qasim plant — the iconic Corolla begins its journey in Pakistan.

1997

Hilux Launch

The legendary Toyota Hilux introduced in Pakistan, becoming the country's most trusted workhorse for business and adventure.

2005

Listed on PSX

Indus Motor Company listed on the Pakistan Stock Exchange, marking a major milestone in transparency and investor confidence.

2015

Fortuner & Corolla Refresh

Introduction of new-generation Fortuner and the all-new Corolla Altis — setting new benchmarks in Pakistani automotive standards.

2023

500,000 Vehicles Milestone

Proudly celebrated delivering over half a million vehicles to Pakistani families — a testament to trust built over three decades.
